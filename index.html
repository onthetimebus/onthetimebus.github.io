Book Reflections

Apr 2026
Days at the Morisaki Bookshop
Days at the Morisaki Bookshop was my first Japanese book and I listened to it on YouTube. It's not a moving book, and the story itself is simple. But that's exactly the beauty of it. There's a warmth to it that stays with you. Listening to it made me want to go to Japan. I would like to visit Tokyo, but also the countryside. A mountain hike, a warm meal somewhere small. Japan is on my list of places to visit, and hopefully I make a trip there soon.
